About LivingEnglish Kids

We don't just teach English — we make children live English.

LivingEnglish Kids is a professional children-focused English learning center based in Tanzania for children aged 4-14 dedicated to helping young learners speak English confidently, clearly and naturally. Our main focus is on empowering children with speaking skills, pronunciation, confidence building, and communication skills in a fun and safe learning environment.

We provide fun, interactive and practical English programs designed to improve communication, confidence, creativity and real-life English use.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of confident English speakers in Tanzania.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, child-friendly English education that fosters confidence, fluency, communication and speaking skills preparing them for global opportunities.
